{# Full Formula Breakdown #}

Rumus Cosine Similarity:

cos(A, B) = (A · B) / (||A|| × ||B||)

Dimana:

  A · B   = Σ(Ai × Bi)            ← dot product

  ||A||  = √(Σ Ai²)             ← norm preference vector

  ||B||  = √(Σ Bi²)             ← norm laptop vector
